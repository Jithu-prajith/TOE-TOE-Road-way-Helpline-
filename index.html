The Critical Gap

Stranded On Indian Highways?

Getting stuck on long national routes is a major safety risk. Professional roadside networks take hours, and traditional mechanics are miles away.

No Nearby Mechanic Shops

Highways span thousands of kilometers of isolated fields. Finding a workshop in rural patches or during late hours is nearly impossible.

Fuel Shortages & Empty Tanks

Miscalculating range between massive highway petrol pumps can strand families without fuel, miles away from the next fuel pump.

Tyre Blowouts & Punctures

Raveling over sharp potholes causes sudden tyre blowouts. Many travelers lack a spare tyre, a working jack, or the physical strength to change it.

Unsafe Isolated Roads

Waiting in darkness on isolated dark highways exposes families to local security hazards, intense night heat, or severe highway elements.

Our Core Concept

A Citizen-Powered Roadside Ecosystem

Toe Toe represents a paradigm shift. We do not connect you to distant, commercial mechanic garages. Instead, we leverage the power of the highway community.

Using real-time GPS location and quick emergency notifications, we connect stranded travelers directly to verified citizens who are just minutes away.

Help fellow drivers going on the same route with tools, jump-start, or extra spares.

Local Villagers & Dhabas

Meet Your Everyday Helpers

Toe Toe coordinates three highly supportive segments of verified active citizens nearby to secure your journey.

Passing Travelers

Local Residents

People living near highway junctions, toll plazas, or dhabas who can fetch support from nearby villages.

"People near highways familiar with the area"

Gig Workers

Registered citizens looking to earn fair secondary income by rapidly delivering petrol or punctured tyre kits.

"Citizens looking to earn while helping travelers"

Safety is our absolute priority. All helper profiles are verified using Aadhaar KYC checks, background verifications, and mobile matching prior to dashboard activation.

Ecosystem Timeline

How Toe Toe Works

A simple 5-step digital lifecycle ensuring swift, transparent assistance in minutes.

01

1. Traveler requests help

Open the app, select your emergency (Breakdown, Puncture, Fuel), and share your automated GPS highway coordinates.

02

2. Helpers receive notification

Our intelligent system broadcasts the request to registered and verified citizens within a 10 km radar radius.

03

3. Helper accepts request

An active helper accepts the job, visualizes your location, and begins navigating toward your live GPS pinpoint.

04

4. Assistance is provided

The helper arrives at your vehicle, delivers fuel, changes the tyre, or helps tow your car to safety.

05

5. Payment & rating completed

Traveler scans the secure simulated UPI QR code to pay, rates the helper's effort, and the helper instantly withdraws earnings.
